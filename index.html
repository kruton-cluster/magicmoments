Founder: Chetan Gowda

Hello, I'm Chetan Gowda, a professional wedding photographer with over 10 years of experience in capturing the beauty and essence of a couple's big day. My passion for photography has led me to create stunning images that are not only visually appealing but also tell the story of the couple's special day.

My style of photography is a combination of artistic and candid shots that truly capture the emotions and candid moments of a wedding. I work closely with my clients to understand their vision and make sure that they are comfortable in front of the camera.
